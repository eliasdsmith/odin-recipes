Tortilla Pizza

Description

This is a simple pizza recipe that you can make when all you have on hand are a tortilla, some cheese, and some pizza sauce.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Place all the toppings on the flour tortilla
  3. Cook tortilla with toppings for 10 minutes and enjoy
