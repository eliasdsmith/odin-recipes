Mississippi Pot Roast

Description

Mississippi pot roast is, as the name suggests, a pot roast recipe that originates from the Southern State of Mississippi. This hearty dish is a good source of iron and stores well as leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast
  • Peperoncinis
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter
  • Various seasonings

Steps

  1. Combine the roast and the seasonings into one crockpot
  2. Cook for 12 hours or longer if needed
  3. That's it, your roast is done and ready to eat
