Sardines with Sun-Dried Tomatoes and Capers

Description

This is a simple recipe for the common Mediterranean dish of sardines and capers. This recipe contains large amounts of vital Omega-3 fatty acids, which promotes both brain and heart health. In addition, it has the benefit of beign cheap and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • One can of Sardines
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 2 tbsp sun-dried tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bread, lightly toasted (optional)

Steps

  1. Drain the canned sardines of any oil
  2. If you are using bread, now is the time to toast it
  3. Mix capers and tomatoes in a small bowl
  4. Add sardines, enjoy by itself or spread on a piece of toast
